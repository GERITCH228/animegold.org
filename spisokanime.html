Anime list

Attack on titan

There is a world that is in no way similar to ours, modern and developing. This world is far from the stereotype that we are used to seeing every day when we get up for a painful job or study. In this "unknown" society for us, which is developing in a strange way, the distant Middle Ages, with its well-established laws and orders, with lords and merchants, is combined with strange people who call themselves "ninja", can sit on the couch and watch an old TV on weekends. Every ninja that lives in this world has conquered three great paths – the path of the body, illusion and hidden – taijutsu, ninjutsu and genjutsu. Each of the disciplines has unique features and special tasks.

Attack on titan 2

The events take place on the territory of Japan, where Ichigo Kurosaki lives. The guy is different from his peers from an early age, because he knows how to communicate with ghosts. One day a young lady, whose name is Rukia Kuchiki, flies into his room. The uninvited guest is a kind of conductor of human souls. The girl is very surprised that the young man has the ability to see her and even touch her. While the stunned heroine languishes in thought, an evil spirit called "empty" suddenly bursts into the room.

Attack on titan 3: part 1

The Taisho era was full of surprises. Many legends found their logical confirmation in those years. People have been thinking about the presence of demons in the thick of the forest for a long time. And these creatures were extremely dangerous and bloodthirsty. At night, they hunted people, killing innocent citizens, demons fed on their flesh. However, over time, these legends began to resemble fairy tales more. There were no similar incidents in the forests, people stopped believing. And it was in vain, because a few years ago, the father of the main character, Tanjiro Kamado, had an accident.
